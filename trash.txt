In this context, personal travel requires additional consideration of health status conditions restrictions, such as whether they have been vaccinated or have a negative 72-hour test result. Personal health monitoring is extremely cumbersome to a certain extent, as it means daily travel requires checking the local real-time epidemic situation, updating travel restrictions en route and obtaining the required health certificates. At the same time, attitudes to COVID-19 vary from region to region. For example, many U.S. states have lifted travel restrictions, while Massachusetts still has vaccination restrictions.
